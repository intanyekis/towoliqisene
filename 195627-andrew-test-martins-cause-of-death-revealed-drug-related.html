Andrew Test Martins Cause Of Death Revealed; Drug Related?

We apologize for the mistake of posting "Adam Martin" instead of "Andrew Martin" in the original story. 

Andrew "Test" Martin’s cause of death was finally revealed to be an accidental overdose, reports the UK Sun.  The Hillsborough County Medical Examiner ruled the death as a result of Oxycodone intoxication.  Oxycodone is the very addictive ingredient found in numerous painkillers, including the popular OxyContin. 

Andrew Martin was found dead by his apartment neighbor n March 14th, 2009.  He had just completed drug rehab, paid for by the WWE, due to his past addictions.  His father and friends have stated in recent interviews that they thought he was doing much better since the rehab.
