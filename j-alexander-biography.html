J. Alexander Bio (Wiki)

Who is J. Alexander?

J. Alexander or Miss J is an American TV personality plus model. J. Alexander rose to fame after working on America’s Next Top Model as a judge, runway coach, and runway trainer.

He was featured in many other shows titled Fashion News Live, The Tyra Banks Show, What Perez Sez, Good Morning America, I Supermodel III, etc. Miss J also wrote a book titled Follow the Model: Miss J’s Guide to Unleashing Presence, Poise, and Power in 2010.

He has also gained a massive number of followers on his official Instagram and Twitter accounts.

J. Alexander- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

His birthdate is 12 April 1958 and he was born in The Bronx, New York City, New York, the United States as Alexander Jenkins. The name of his mother is Mary and his father’s identity was Julius.

Further, J. Alexander’s ancestry is All-American and as per his academics, he attended college to become an accountant.

J. Alexander- Professional Life, Career

J. Alexander first started as a model and was signed by Elite Model Management in his teenage years. He before settling in Paris worked in Tokoyo for around three years.

He is also a talented modeling coach who has trained famous personalities such as Naomi Campbell, Kimora Lee Simmons, Nadja Auermann, Claudia Mason, and Julia Stegner. The designers he helped are Hervé Léger, Lars Nilsson, Bill Blass, Valentino, John Galliano, Chanel, etc.
