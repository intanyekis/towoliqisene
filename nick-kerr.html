Nick Kerr is the son of a professional basketball coach and a former player Steve Kerr who is the head coach of the Golden State Warriors. 

Nick attended Torrey Pines High School and earned his bachelor’s degree in business administration from the University of San Diego. 

Nick played basketball for years as a point guard, following his father’s footsteps but could not make it to the NBA like his father. 

He became an Intern Coach at the University of California. Later, he took the job at San Antonio Spurs as a film assistant.

His parents met on a blind date

His parents Steve and Margot Kerr, both attended the University of Arizona in 1985. They met on a blind date arranged by their mutual friend, Bruce Fraser, who played with Steve in the College basketball team. 

Bruce and Steve are still working together. Bruce is the assistant coach for Golden State Warrior. 

Both Steve and Margot were nerdy, which connected them and soon got along and began dating. 

They even remained in a long-distance relationship when Margot was working in Los Angeles. After Steve was drafted by the Phoenix Suns, she gave up on her job to be with Steve. 

They ended up marrying on the 15th of September, 1990. The couple has been inseparable since then.

He has two younger siblings

Nick, who was born Nicolas, has two younger siblings, a sister Madeleine (Maddy) Kerr, and a brother Matthew Kerr. 

His sister is an athlete as well. She is an avid basketball player. She was ranked at the top in her senior year atthe University of California. 

However, she later joined the law school at Berkeley. The younger brother Matthew is not into any of the sports. 

He is into writing and was a film student. All three siblings live on their own. They moved back to their parent’s home in San Diego during the Coronavirus pandemic to be with one another. 

According to Steve, the Kerr family got enough time to spend quality time with each other during the pandemic. 

He also said they took advantage of all the time together since they do not get to spend much time together due to their busy schedules.
