Drew Van Acker is an American actor, model, and producer who is known for playing Ian Archer in the 13-episode live-action teen science-fiction television series Tower Prep, Jason DiLaurentis in the teen drama mystery thriller television series Pretty Little Liars, Detective Tommy Campbell in the CBS crime thriller series Training Day, and Remi Delatour in the television comedy-drama and mystery series Devious Maids. Moreover, the actor has also been cast in many other shows and movies such as Spy Intervention, Fortress, Castle, Last Survivors, Hell’s Kitchen, Greek, Titans, Pretty Dirty Secrets, and Camouflage.

Education

Drew Van Acker studied at Shawnee High School, a 4-year comprehensive public high school in Burlington County, New Jersey. During his time at the school, he started participating in high school drama classes and played on the school’s soccer and lacrosse teams.

He then attended Towson University in Maryland on a soccer scholarship. While in the university, Drew took a number of theater courses in order to refocus on acting.
