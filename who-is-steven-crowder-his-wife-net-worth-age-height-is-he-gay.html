Who Is Steven Crowder? His Wife, Net Worth, Age, Height, Is He Gay?

Steven Crowder is an American-Canadian political commentator who also doubles as a comedian and an actor. Politics maybe be a boring topic to some people but many others, Steve Crowder inclusive, have chosen to fully participate in the happenings, permutations, and predictions in the world of politics.

He is not just a conservative political analyst but is famed for being the host of Louder with Crowder, a television show that covers, pop culture and politics on BlazeTV. He was also a former correspondent for Fox News.

Who Is Steven Crowder? (Age)

Steven Blake Crowder was born on the 7th day of June 1987 in Grosse Pointe, Michigan to Darrin S. Crowder and Francine Crowder. Growing up, the family relocated to Greenfield Park, Quebec, Canada where he was bred. He was born into a Christian family.

As for his educational pursuit, he was educated at Centennial Regional High School, Longueuil, in Canada before proceeding for further studies at Champlain College, Vermont, the US.

He had his first contact with showbiz s a boy when he got a voice role on a children’s television series Arthur. As he clocked 17, he began to perform as a stand-up comedian in various places. By the time he was 18, he had performed in the prestigious comedy stage Just for Laughs, making him the youngest comedian to do so.

Other films and television appearances that Steven Crowder has made include his screen debut film Two Summers (2001), 3 Needles (2005), The Secret (2007), To Save a Life (2009) where he assumed the role of Doug Moore, and A YouTube Carol (2017).

He became a regular feature in XM Radio where there he got booked to perform comedy sketches. The talented comedian later became a panelist in News Media when he was 21. His outstanding contribution earned him a job with the Fox News Channel where he worked from 2009 till 2012.

With his fame grown globally, he created a YouTube channel, LouderWithCrowder where he posts his political opinions, satiric political responses, news videos, among others. The channel created on June 2, 2006, now commands over 3.5 million subscribers with over 820 video uploads.

Crowder has over time with his conservative commentary attracted accolades to himself as well as criticism. Alongside some members of American For Prosperity, he made the headlines in December 2012 when both parties got into altercations over the Right To Work Bill passed by the government which escalated into a punch exchange. Charges were however not pressed by anyone after the footage of the event was released.

On the other hand, he fell out with his former employer, the Fox News after he made a negative comment against one of its program host, Sean Hannity. He continued with political activities in different conservative platforms including the Conservative Political Action Conference (CPAC).

His podcast LouderWithCrowder was set up in 2017 and streams on SoundCloud, iTunes as well as terrestrial radio. His Meme Change My Mind was voted as the 18th best meme in 2018 according to the NewYork-based Media for Youth Culture – Complex.

Net Worth

Steven Crowder has made name and money for himself in comedy and political commentary since his foray began in his early twenties. All this has added to his net worth estimated to be $3 miilion. His income is also generated from his YouTube channel that has over 3.5 million subscribers.

Is He Gay?

The political analyst and comedian has been a subject of many speculations with regards to his sexuality. He was once alleged to be gay, however, he never came forward to accept or debunk the rumors. Until something concrete comes up, the gay rumors that have plagued him can only be said to be unfounded.

Wife

The renowned actor is a proud husband. He is married to Hilary Korzon Crowder. They tied the knot in August of 2012. Hilary Korzon was a student at Calvin College. She is an interior decorator and brand sales representative, market strategist and promoter for Leland International. The couple do not have any child yet, but they have a dog named Hopper that Steven loves and often flaunts on his social media pages.

Height
