Who Is Zachariah Branch? Wikipedia Explored

Zachariah Branch is a wide receiver for Bishop Gorman High School, Las Vegas, Nevada. He ranks as the country’s No. 1 wide receiver and the top overall prospect in Nevada.

Elite 2023 junior wide receiver helped Bishop Gorman win its eleventh state title in 13 seasons.
