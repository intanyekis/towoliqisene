Early Life

Born Shawn Peter Raul Mendes on August 8, 1998, in Pickering, Ontario, Canada, Mendes embarked on his musical journey at a young age, honing his craft by posting song covers on Vine in 2013. His mesmerizing renditions garnered widespread acclaim, catapulting him to fame and catching the attention of industry insiders. Securing a record deal with Island Records in 2014, Mendes swiftly rose to prominence with his debut major label EP and studio album, “Handwritten,” both of which achieved critical acclaim and commercial success. His chart-topping singles, including “Stitches,” propelled him into the spotlight, earning him accolades and recognition as one of Time’s “25 Most Influential Teens” and “The 30 Most Influential Teens.”

Shawn Mendes Career

Mendes’ musical prowess continued to captivate audiences worldwide with subsequent albums like “Illuminate” and his self-titled album, each garnering widespread acclaim and reinforcing his status as a musical prodigy.
