FOX's hit animated show, Bob's Burgers, won the heart of thousands of viewers with its quirky and out of the ordinary tales of the Belcher family. Bob's Burgers is a down on its luck burger restaurant owned by third-generation restauranteur, Bob Belcher. While Bob has his own charm, it's his family that adds some spice to his life. His wife Linda is a flamboyant, wine-loving, exuberant woman who supports her family no matter what.

His children are the real pot of gold. Their eldest daughter Tina is like any young teen obsessed with boys, erotic fiction and butts. Gene is the middle child who is has a zest for life and fart sounds. Louise, on the other hand, is the evil mastermind with pink bunny ears.

Andy Kindler: $500 Thousand

Andy Kindler plays a recurring role on the show for the past ten seasons as Mort, the mortician. Kindler is an American comedian who often lends his voice for voice acting. Kindler has had roles in other works done by the creator of Bob's Burgers, producer Loren Bouchard. For example, he has been a voice actor in Dr. Katz with Laua Silverman and the show Home Movies.

Kindler is also played the character "Andy" on the show Everyone Loves Raymond. he was a friend fo the show's protagonist Raymond Barone.

Larry Murphy: $1 Million

Larry Murphy plays more than one character on Bob's Burgers. One of his recurring roles is Teddy the handyman who eats a cheeseburger every day at Bob's restaurant. He is almost your stereotypical handyman who wears a blue button-up, has hair arms and legs, and shows the occasion buttcrack. It's also very rare to see him without his hat. He also voices Javed Fazel, Edith Cranwinkle and Gretchen on the show.

Murphy has also voiced Lt. Francis Grimes on the show Ugly Americans. In the 2007 show WordGirl, Murphy voiced various side characters including Amazing Rope Guy

Dave, Principal, Reporter and ZooKeeper.

John Roberts: $1 Million

Playing family matriarch in the Belcher family is Linda. Linda is played by voice actor, actor, comedian, writer and singer John Roberts. Roberts has admitted that the inspiration behind the voice of Linda came from mimicking his mother. Linda is a doting mother who supports her children no matter the odd behaviors or plans they have. She is also highly supportive of Bob's dream of being a restauranteur.

Roberts has done voice acting for other shows including Archer, The Simpsons, Gravity Falls and The Awesome's. In 2019, he released a song titled "Looking".

Eugene Mirman: $2 Million

Believe it or not, Eugen Mirman plays Gene in Bob's Burgers. Gene is the rambunctious and over the top middle child who is very confident in who he is. Mirman is also a comedian who has done stand-up comedy. He performed on John Oliver's New York Stand Up Show in 2010 for Comedy Central. Mirman even has a stand-up special on Netflix titled Eugene Mirman: Vegan on His Way to the Complain Store.

Mirman lent his voice to portray the character Cecil Tunt in FX's Archer. In the Netflix original, The Adventures of Puss in Boots, Mirman helped voice the character, Emperor Keith Merman. The character has a funny resemblance to Mirman's real last name.

Dan Mintz: $ 2 Million

Playing the boy crazy and zombie loving Tina is played by comedian and voice actor, Dan Mintz. Tina is like any young woman coming into her own who becomes obsessed with boys and has a fascination with zombies and erotic fiction. She often combines the two. Mintz voices the Tine with a shy straightforward voice.

Mintz has made his way into the entertainment business with various voice actor roles. For three episodes he played T.V. on Adventure Time. He played his role as Tina in an episode of The Simpsons. Mintz also appeared in Silicon Valley, The Goldbergs and Speechless.

David Herman: $ 3 Million

David Herman plays guidance counselor Philip Frond in Bob's Burgers. Frond believes in talking about your feelings with hand crotcheted emotional puppets. He also becomes a sort of nemesis to Louise Belcher. Herman is also known for playing other television characters like Scruffy the janitor in the show Futurama. In Beavis and Butt-head, he also portrayed additional voices.

Herman is the main role voicing Kevin Crawford in the web television series Paradise PD (2018). Back in 1999, Herman played Michael Bolton in the comedy film Office Space. In the animated comedy film about a young honey bee titled Bee Movie, Herman voiced Buzz, Bob Bumble and Pilot.

Kristen Schaal: $5 Million

Louise Belcher is the youngest Belcher child who is often responsible for a lot of pranks and her family's misadventures. Louise is voiced by actress, voice actress, comedian, and writer Kristen Schaal. Schaal is also noted for playing Sarah Lynn on BoJack Horseman that got her nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award. Outside of voice acting, Schaal has appeared in many on-screen roles.

H.Jon Benjamin: $6 Million

Retrauatneur Bob Belcher is voiced by H.Jon Benjamin. Bob Belcher has hairy arms, a bushy mustache and has to manage a restaurant always on the brink of closing. Not to mention keep his eye on his children who often rope him and Linda into trouble. Benjamin, besides voicing Bob, is famous for voicing Sterling Archer in Archer. Benjamin's voice is distinguishable no matter what role he plays.

He plays Carl Graves in Family Guy and Satan in Lucy, The Daugther of the Devil for eleven episodes. He was also a writer for the series. Benjamin starred in the series Jon Benjamin Has a Van. He co-created the show with stand-up comedian and writer Leo Allen. If you're a fan of Sex and the City, you could find him make a guest appearance in the episode "Bay of Married Pigs". He also shows up in the Arby's tv commercials.

Sarah Silverman: $12 Million

Sarah Silverman is a  stand-up comedian, actor, singer, producer, and writer who has done a lot of work in the span of her career. Her roles include playing recurring characters Ollie Pesto and Ms.Schnur in Bob's Burgers. Silverman's list of on-screen roles is roughly two pages long. She has roles in the films Wreck-It Ralph as Vanellope von Schweetz and School of Rock with Jack Black.

On Saturday Night Live, she appeared in eighteen episodes and was also a writer. Silverman made appearances in Seinfeld, Monk, Furuama, Louie, Masters of Sex and The Simpsons. In 2007, she starred in the comedy central show The Sarah Silverman Program as herself. She was also a writer, executive producer and co-creator for the show.

Megan Mullally: $15 Million

Ironically the Bob's Burgers actor with the highest net worth is also the one who makes the least money in the show. Megan Mullally plays Gayle, Linda Belcher's sister. Gayle represents an aunt every family has. She is a devoted cat lady, even though the cats hate her. She lives in a small studio apartment, can't hold a solid job and is frugal.

Mullally is known for playing Karen Walker in the Tv sitcom Will & Grace. She has won two Emmy's for her role as a supporting actress and winning three SAG awards. She has appeared on How I Met Your Mother and Frasier. Mullally was the host of the talk show The Megan Mullally for 71 episodes. She also played Chief in Children's Hospital and appeared on 30 Rock. For nine episodes, Mullally was Tammy Swanson II on Parks and Recreation.
