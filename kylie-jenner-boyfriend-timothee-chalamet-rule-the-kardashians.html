Kylie Jenner Denies Fans Access To Her Relationship As She Sets A Rule For Timothee Chalamet On The

Highlights

  • Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet are keeping their relationship strictly off-limits from The Kardashians on Hulu.
  • Despite attending events together, the couple was noticeably absent from the premiere episode of Season 5.
  • Kylie and Timothée's private romance timeline started in January 2023 and has sparked both pregnancy and breakup rumors.

Season 5 of The Kardashians is back on Hulu, showing more of the busy lives of the Kardashian-Jenners. The love lives of the famous family are a primary interest, especially that of the most private of the bunch, Kylie Jenner. According to The Daily Mail, Jenner has been dating actor Timothée Chalamet since January 2023, but they have shared very little about their relationship. Unfortunately, Jenner shared a strict rule about their relationship that surprised fans.

Although the Kardashian-Jenners show most aspects of their lives on social media, The Kardashians show the ins and outs of their day-to-day, romantic relationships, and the highs and lows of their family. Kylie Jenner’s privacy around her relationship has only made fans want to know more about what she's hiding. Keep scrolling to learn what strict rule Kylie Jenner implemented and her private relationship timeline with Timothée!

Kylie Jenner's Strict Rule About Timothée Chalamet On The Kardashians

Understandably, fans of the Kardashians and Jenners want to see the romantic relationships among the family. However, if fans are waiting to hear more between Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet, they’ll be waiting a long time. It appears that Jenner’s rule for filming was that her relationship with Chalamet was off-limits.

The premiere episode of Season 5 of The Kardashians didn’t show Jenner and Chalamet together. Chalamet wasn’t even mentioned. This was surprising considering the premiere episode centered around Paris Fashion Week, and the family is theoretically supposed to show the reality of their lives. Jenner and Chalamet weren’t just dating during Paris Fashion Week, but they attended famous shows and dinners together, as noted by Elle.

Kylie Jenner And Timothée Chalamet’s Relationship Timeline Has Been Secretive

In the winter of 2022, Harper’s Bazaar shared that Kylie Jenner and her longtime partner Travis Scott had broken up. The two have had an on-and-off again relationship for five years and share two children together, Stormi (6) and Aires (2). The reason for their split was never confirmed.

However, Jenner wasn’t single for long. Teen Vogue reported that Kylie and Timothée Chalamet sparked romance rumors in January 2023. The connection between Jenner and Chalamet was an unexpected one for fans. With Chalamet being a private movie star and Jenner being an open reality star, their union was a surprise.

In the past, Jenner has never been with an actor of Chalamet's celebrity. The actor is most known for his roles in Dune, Wonka, and Lady Bird.

According to Glamour, sparks were flying when Jenner and Chalamet were seen talking at the Jean Paul Gaultier show for the 2023 Paris Fashion Week. They were seen laughing with each other and genuinely enjoying one another’s company. It's presumed this was the first time Jenner and Chalamet met.

In April of that same year, Jenner and Chalamet were seen for the first time since Fashion Week getting tacos together. An insider for Entertainment Tonight shared the two were newly dating and keeping things super casual.

“It’s not serious, but Kylie is enjoying hanging out with Timothée and seeing where it goes. It’s been really fun for her because it feels a lot different than her past relationships. It’s new and exciting for Kylie and she’s having a lot of fun.”

Proof of the couple's intimacy hit an all-time high when they were seen at Beyoncé’s concert in Los Angeles. The two stayed close to one another and were seen dancing and kissing throughout the concert. Carrying on their public outings, they were then seen canoodling at that year’s US Open, as reported by InStyle.

Because the couple is notoriously private, a source told Life & Style that despite being on The Kardashians, they're still very much together.

"Kylie and Timmy are still together, in love and it's serious,”

Since Kylie Jenner's previous relationships were long-lasting, it's presumed that she's in it for the long haul with Timothée Chalamet and it's not a casual romance any longer.
