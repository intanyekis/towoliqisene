Kim K., Kanye West Wear Matching Acid Wash Jeans: Airport Style Photos

The couple that wears acid wash jeans together, stays together! Kim Kardashian and Kanye West sported matching takes on the ’80s staple as they landed at LAX on Thursday, May 19.

Kardashian, 35, styled her jeans with a nude bodysuit, a gold belt and olive lace-up booties. Saint Laurent shades, a black Hermes purse and a camel coat completed her airport ensemble.

Meanwhile, her husband, 38, kept things slightly more casual by adding a white tee and Yeezy Boosts (and, despite his usually stoic facade, the occasional smile too). With just days to go until their second wedding anniversary on May 24, the couple, who share North, 2, and Saint, 6 months, strolled through the airport arm in arm.
