Check Out This Sweet Family Photo Lance Gross Shared of His Wife &amp; Kids Celebrating Easter

Lance Gross had the perfect Easter holiday with his family and took to Instagram to share lovely photos with his fans. The "House of Payne" actor shares two kids, Berkeley and Lennon, with his stunning wife, Rebecca.

As Hollywood celebs marked Easter over the weekend, social media platforms were filled with many adorable family photos, including those of "House of Payne" actor Lance Gross and his sweet family.

Lance is the doting father of two beautiful children, Berkeley and Lennon Gross, who he shares with his wife, Rebecca Gross. The family-of-four spent the Easter holidays together and posed for two sweet snapshots which were shared on Instagram.

Lance Gross poses at the Canelo Alvarez and Gennady “GGG” Golovkin press tour on June 22, 2017 in Los Angeles, California. | Source: Getty Images

AN EASTER FAMILY PHOTO

The first photo featured Berkeley and Lennon sporting wide smiles as they posed in front of an Easter bunny and pink building festively decorated with colorful eggs, carrots, and flowers.

Both kids were dressed in smart casual attire, with Berkeley rocking a blue and white striped golf shirt, jeans, and white sneakers, and Lennon looking handsome in a white knit jersey, cream pants, and sneakers.

In the second snap, Lance and Rebecca joined their kids for a family photo. The "Our Family Wedding" star and his wife were all smiles as they sat on either side of the wooden bench with Berkeley between them and Lennon on Rebecca's lap.

Lance was clad in a short-sleeved denim shirt, white ripped jeans, and colorful sneakers, while Rebecca looked chic in a camel outfit paired with stylish black boots.

LANCE'S PROUD FATHER MOMENTS

The Easter family photo is only one of many heartwarming photos that Lance has shared on his Instagram. Time and time again, he has proven his pride and devotion to his family by publicly chronicling some of their memorable moments.

When he and Rebecca welcomed Lennon in 2018, the "Temptation: Confessions of a Marriage Counselor," actor paraded his son on social media and even expressed his excitement that his son shared his brown complexion.

PANDEMIC PARENTING CHALLENGES

Lance and Rebecca take their jobs as parents seriously, but amid the COVID-19 pandemic, they faced new parenthood challenges which forced them to rediscover a balance between family, career, and personal time.

The couple told the New York Times that self-care was their main key to successful pandemic parenting. Lance found fulfillment in his creative pursuits while Rebecca enjoyed tailgating with her friends.

Additionally, Rebecca and Lance continued to nurture their relationship by prioritizing their date nights which included visiting a drive-in movie theater while their nanny watched the kids at home.
