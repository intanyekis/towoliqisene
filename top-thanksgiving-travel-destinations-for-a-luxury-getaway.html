The Top Thanksgiving Travel Destinations For A Luxury Getaway

Highlights

  • New York City offers a luxurious Thanksgiving getaway with extravagant celebrations, fine dining, and breathtaking views, costing between $5,000 to $10,000 or more.
  • Paris, the city of love, provides a dream-like experience with a cruise dinner, cultural attractions, and luxury accommodations, ranging from $5,000 to $10,000.
  • Celebrating Thanksgiving in Los Cabos or Maui allows for a stunning and romantic experience with lavish resorts, activities, and fine dining, costing between $4,000 to $10,000.

Thanksgiving offers an opportunity to take a break from your bustling routine and spend time with your loved ones, arranging get togethers, hosting happy feasts at home, and making the most out of this special event.

Why don’t you make this Thanksgiving worthy enough to be a prominent part of your journal when you take your family to the most extravagant locations, stay at the most luxurious hotels, and consume the most scrumptious foods?

Let's take a virtual journey of the top Thanksgiving travel destinations for a luxury gateway.

10 New York City, USA - A Grand Thanksgiving Parade

When it comes to a luxury getaway for Thanksgiving, New York City tops the list, and Manhattan is widely known for its lavish holiday celebrations. Consider staying in a hotel room with a view of the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade.

For a fine dining experience, you can pamper yourself with a Thanksgiving feast at a vast array of gourmet restaurants (most with holiday prix fixe menus) – at a cost of around $150 to $250 for each person. Moreover, you can enjoy a Thanksgiving cruise dinner on the Hudson River.

A helicopter tour would be a luxury and once-in-a-lifetime Thanksgiving experience to view the city from different perspectives and enjoy the Big Apple’s vibrant energy.

The cost for a luxury Thanksgiving gateway to New York City ranges between $5,000 to $10,000 – or even higher, for a more lavish experience (or with a Black Friday shopping spree at any of the city's high-end boutiques and department stores).

9 Paris, France - A Thanksgiving In The City Of Love

Spending Thanksgiving in the city of love is like a dream come true. A cruise dinner along the Seine River would be an unforgettable and classic experience as it offers breathtaking views of the Eiffel Tower, Notre Dame Cathedral, and other beautiful landmarks of the city.

If you’re planning to experience such a magnificent dinner, do not hesitate to spend $200 to $400. Luxury accommodations (such as in the Ritz Paris) can cost $2,000 to $5,000, depending on the level of luxury of the hotel.

You can visit the world's largest museum, the Louvre, for a unique cultural experience. The total cost for a luxury Thanksgiving in Paris would be around $5,000 to $10,000 – or even more, depending upon the level of luxury visitors wish to experience in the City of Light.

Total Cost On The Low End:

$5,000

Total Cost On The High End

$10,000

8 Los Cabos, Mexico - A Luxury Paradise

Celebrating Thanksgiving in Los Cabos is itself a luxurious and stunning experience. Famous for its lavish resorts, romantic beaches, and dazzling nightlife, Los Cabos will enhance your Thanksgiving experience with its well known One & Only Palmilla resort – which costs approximately $1,500 to $2,000 per night.

Moreover, you can indulge in activities like fishing, golfing, and spa treatments for an amazing and memorable experience. If you’ve got $4,000 to $8,000 in your savings, a Thanksgiving trip to Los Cabos is particularly ideal for a romantic two-person getaway.

Total Cost On The Low End:

$4,000

Total Cost On The High End

$8,000

7 Maui, Hawaii - A Tropical Paradise For Two

Maui is an ideal spot for a couple looking for a luxury and romantic Thanksgiving escape. Maui offers scenic beaches, lush landscapes, luxury accommodations, and fine dining at a veritable constellation of restaurants.

Stays at luxurious resorts such as Four Seasons Resort Maui can range from $1,000 to $2,000 for a single night. Consider a private Thanksgiving Luau filled with Hula dancing, music, and special Hawaiian food in an exclusive setting for a lavish experience; it can cost around $1,500 or more.

The total cost for a luxury Thanksgiving escape to Maui can be from $4,000 to $10,000 for a couple.

Total Cost On The Low End:

$4,000

6 Dubai, UAE - A Modern Oasis

How about spending Thanksgiving in a futuristic city situated in the desert – where millions of tourists come to experience its sumptuous lifestyle and skyscraping buildings, including Burj Al Arab, Armani Hotel Dubai, and The Palm? Staying at one of these five-star hotels would cost around $1,000 to $2,000.

You cannot miss a helicopter tour to experience this city’s magnificent view, including Palm Jumeirah, World’s Island, and Burj ul Khalifa, the world’s largest building. In the city of malls, you can take your family shopping for the most luxurious brands ... just don’t forget to check the price tag before buying potentially expensive souvenirs.

The total cost for a luxury Thanksgiving escape to Dubai can be from $5,000 to $15,000.

5 Barcelona, Spain - A Feast For The Senses

Barcelona provides a unique and exclusive setting for a luxury gateway, owing to its fine dining, culinary delights, and artistic architecture. Staying in luxury accommodations here can cost up to $1,000.

To garnish your experience, visit the city’s one of the finest masterpieces, Park Guell. Thanksgiving Michelin Star Dining can cost from $200 to $300.

The total expense of staying and spending a luxury Thanksgiving in Spain can range from $3,000 to $5,000.

4 St. Moritz, Switzerland - A Winter Luxury

Total Cost: Approximately $3,500 to $10,000

Celebrating Thanksgiving in St. Moritz is like visiting a real life winter wonderland. Consider staying at one of the iconic luxury Alpine resorts, which cost somewhere between $500 to $1,000.

You can indulge yourself in activities like winter sports, gourmet dining, skiing, and spa treatments for an exclusive experience. The total cost for a luxury gateway to St. Moritz will be $3,500 to $10,000, depending upon your desires and other expenses.

Spending Thanksgiving in a beautiful Alpine destination would be a unique and old world experience.

Total Cost On The Low End:

$3,500

Total Cost On The High End

$10,000

3 Kyoto, Japan - Tranquility In Tradition

Total Cost: Approximately $1,500 to $3,000

Kyoto is an ancient city in the heart of Japan. Thanksgiving celebrations in Kyoto offer rich and traditional Japanese culture. Accommodation in high-end Ryokans and luxury hotels would cost approximately $250 to $550.

2 Bora Bora, French Polynesia - Over Water Bungalow Bliss

Total Cost: Approximately $7,000 to $20,000

Bora Bora in French Polynesia is a perfect location for those seeking luxury and a pampered Thanksgiving. This lavish gateway begins with accommodation in the world's most iconic bungalows and villas, many of which are situated atop crystal waters, and can cost around $2,000 or even more for a single night.

You can experience private beach dinners and island picnics for an exclusive lavish experience. The total expense for a luxury gateway in French Polynesia will cost around $7,000 to $20,000. Thanksgiving in Bora Bora is not just a holiday; it's a trip to Paradise.

1 Santorini, Greece - Bask In The Mediterranean Sea

A Thanksgiving celebration in Santorini would be a luxurious experience. At an expense of $200 to $600, gourmet dining with a scenic view of Caldera at one of the upscale restaurants will certainly inspire a deep sense of gratitude.

Private sunset meals and spa treatments are available as additional flourishes. A luxury Thanksgiving gateway to Santorini, Greece, could easily run visitors from $3,000 to $7,000.

From a romantic Thanksgiving in Paris to the tranquility of traditions in Kyoto, from the Winterland of Spain to the scenic sunsets of Santorini, each of these destinations offers a unique, luxurious, and memorable Thanksgiving experience.
