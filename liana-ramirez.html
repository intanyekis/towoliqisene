Liana Ramirez is an American actress, dancer, choreographer, author, social media personality, YouTuber, model, and TikTok star. She is known for playing Cybervillain Roxy and Robo-Roxy in Power Rangers Beast Morphers, the 26th and 27th seasons of the American television program Power Rangers, between 2019 and 2020. Moreover, Liana has also been a part of projects such as Super Impossible Squad, Another Assembly, Destruction Los Angeles, A Christmas Wish, Lab Rats: Bionic Island, June Born in May, May I Die, Star Light, Criminal Minds, Ugly Sweet, and The Goldbergs.
