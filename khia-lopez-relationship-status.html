American Instagram Model Khia Lopez Finding Love At The Young Age Of 14 And Influencing Social Media

In the current day and age, social media influencers and Instagram models are crushing it. Such is Khia Lopez, who already has bagged deals from top model agencies because of her modeling on Instagram despite her age of 14.

As the saying goes, talent always finds its way to come on top. We've seen evidence of it in many movies and TV series. Jaden Smith and Odessa Adlon are some prime examples. As Khia is getting famous each day, her fan base is growing, with many wanting to know a bit more about her personal life. 

Khia Is Already In a Relationship

At the age of sixteen, many of Khia's fans were not expecting her to be in a relationship already. Scrolling through her Instagram, we can see the comments from the people asking about her boyfriend in disbelief.

If you're among the same tribe of people, then allow us to clarify about her romantic affair. Yes, the fashion model is currently dating a guy named Cristian Sanchez. Apart from his name, not many details can is found as he is not an active user of Social media. 

According to Lopez's Instagram posts, the adorable couple started dating from August 2019. The first picture of the couple is seen on her Instagram on August 6, 2019. In addition to that, there are many more images where the duo is cozying up with each other in exotic locations. 

Her Mother Manages Her Social Media

Khia started her Instagram account in 2014, and since then, her account is managed by her mother, Tiffany Palladine. Khia was only nine years old at the time because of which her mother took the responsibility to manage her daughter's profile.

Tiffany posted pictures of Khia in her beautiful dresses, which went viral on the internet. The Insta pictures made her famous overnight, which helped her secure some big brand fashion deals.

During that time, Lopez got very popular on another social media platform called TikTok. In TikTok, she gained around 937.9 thousand followers, making her one of the big stars on the social media platform.

Lopez is Now a Social Media Influencer

With each passing day, her followers on her social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram started to reach new heights. As of now, she has more than 232,000 followers on Instagram, where she posts regularly about her lifestyle and fashion choices.

After her soaring rise to fame, she signed deals with fashion brands like KidzKandi, Weekend Wardrobe, and Little Trendsetter, to name a few. We can see her wearing amazing dresses from these brands in her pics. 

Lopez is just starting as a social media influencer, and with time, she can reach the fame of some of her counterparts like Desiree Montoya and Angie Varona, to name a few. Desiree and Angie both have more than half a million followers on social media platforms.
