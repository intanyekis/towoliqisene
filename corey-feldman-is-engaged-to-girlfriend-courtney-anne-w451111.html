Corey Feldman Is Engaged to Girlfriend Courtney Anne

Congrats! Actor Corey Feldman is engaged to his longtime girlfriend and Corey’s Angels bandmate Courtney Anne, Us Weekly can confirm.

The former 80s child star and his Canadian girlfriend are “getting married very soon,” a source tells Us.

The Lost Boys actor and Anne, 27, are celebrating their engagement with a joint bachelor and bachelorette party in Las Vegas at the Chateau on Saturday, November 19. 

The 45-year-old tweeted a link to details of their big bash as he thanked fans for their support over the happy news on Thursday, November 17.

“Thank U all 4 UR Messages! @CourteneyAnne12 & I feel quite blessed 2 hav all of ur [heart]!” he wrote. “Now join us! Come celebr8 n Vegas!”

In September, The Goonies actor and his girlfriend faced criticism and ridicule after their performance of his new single ‘Go 4 It’ on the Today show.

Feldman, who said the harsh comments left him in tears, later spoke out against online trolls and received public support from celebrities including singer Pink and his former Stand By Me costar Jerry O’Connell, as well as an outpouring of well wishes from fans on social media.

The actor told Us at the time that he would have harmed himself over the negative comments if he hadn’t faced a public backlash before.
